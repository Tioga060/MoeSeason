Rules

Current Season: Preseason

Sessions

  • At Least {{rules.sessionMin}} games per session
  • {{rules.sessionsRequired}} sessions in a tank required for rank
  • Sessions are pulled every 15 minutes, so it is possible to get more than the minimum games compiled into a session
  • Sessions do not necessarily mean 1 sitting, they are ~{{rules.sessionMin}} games over any time frame pulled after the {{rules.sessionMin}}th is played
  • Your best {{rules.sessionsRequired}} sessions by exp/game are averaged together when your rank is calculated
  • Games per session and sessions required for rank are tuned in order to reduce the likelihood that a bad player will win a tank because of a lucky unbeatable session

MoeScores

  • Individual MoeScores are calculated using your rank in a given category: {{rules.moecalcstring}}
  • These scores are then divided by the maximum total score possible in a tank by achieving rank 1 in every category to normalize the total score between 0 and 100
  • Finally, they are multiplied by their respective multipliers below and added to each other to create the total score for a tank
  • Total tank moescores are then multiplied by the tank's overall multiplier before added to your overall score
  • This way, more competitive tanks will give you a higher score, so the person in first place isn't just someone who gets first place on all of the hipster tanks
  • The MoeScore calculation function is designed to reward higher ranks by using an exponential scale instead of a linear scale, so you get much more points for being in first than second, and so on
  • Multipliers will typically be tuned in a way that makes sense for the tank. Heavy tanks will have less emphasis on spots/game, for example, and artillery will have a higher emphasis on damage and kills
  • Moescores are calculated every 15 minutes

Signatures

  • Signatures will pick one of your highest rank tanks and will display each individual ranking, along with your overall site rank
  • Left column, top to bottom: damage per game rank, spots per game rank, kills per game rank
  • Right column, top to bottom: experience per game rank, winrate rank, overall tank rank
  • Signatures are updated once an hour

Note

All rules are flexible and open to input, if you have complaints or ideas, please share them in the comments
